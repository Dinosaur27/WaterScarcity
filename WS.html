What is water scarcity?

Since 2007 we have experienced a growing issue of both physical and economical water scarcity. Water scarcity is a worldwide health crisis that affects areas where water was previously hard to come by. Nearly one million people die from sanitation and hygiene related diseases each year. Water as we all know, is a very important part of survival since it provides a way to maintain a healthy hygiene, hydration, and the production of agriculture for our consumption.
